About Me

Before Galvanize

Before I started this new journey of becoming a Web Developer I spent that past 10 years working in healthcare. I found myself always wanting solutions to our slow/terrible UX software and equipment. This is what made me want to go into software development. I want to ease the work flow of Dr’s, nurses, techs, and other providers. Working with people in their worst days is a very humbling experience and makes you feel like you are impacting people’s lives. During some of the most critical times in the hospital you have equipment not working or having to leave the bed side to chart. I hope to change the future of how healthcare providers use the technology at the bedside.
